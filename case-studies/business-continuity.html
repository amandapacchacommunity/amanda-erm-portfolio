Business Continuity

Review of resilience planning and disruption-readiness practices supporting operational continuity.

Objective

Assess continuity readiness and identify improvements that support faster recovery and stronger operational resilience.

Key Findings

  • Critical process dependencies were not fully documented
  • Recovery priorities were unclear
  • Continuity testing was limited

Recommendations

  • Identify and document critical processes
  • Define recovery priorities and owners
  • Run continuity exercises on a routine basis

Skills Demonstrated

Continuity planning, resilience assessment, dependency mapping, and disruption response review.
