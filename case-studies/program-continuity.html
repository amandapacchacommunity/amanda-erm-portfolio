Program Continuity

Assessment of disruption response planning and continuity strategies supporting program delivery.

Objective

Review readiness for operational disruption and evaluate service continuity strategies.

Key Findings

  • Continuity plans were incomplete
  • Operational dependencies were undocumented
  • Response procedures varied across teams

Recommendations

  • Create continuity playbooks
  • Document operational dependencies
  • Conduct disruption response exercises

Skills Demonstrated

Business continuity planning, operational risk assessment, and resilience strategy analysis.
