Governance Controls

Evaluation of governance structures, internal controls, and oversight mechanisms supporting institutional accountability.

Objective

Assess governance practices that support operational transparency, decision-making accountability, and institutional oversight.

Key Findings

  • Oversight roles were inconsistently documented
  • Board reporting structures varied across departments
  • Escalation pathways were not clearly defined

Recommendations

  • Define governance reporting standards
  • Clarify oversight and escalation responsibilities
  • Document board-level reporting processes

Skills Demonstrated

Governance analysis, internal control review, institutional oversight evaluation.
