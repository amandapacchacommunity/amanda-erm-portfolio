Control Monitoring

Assessment of issue tracking, exception handling, and operational control quality in finance settings.

Objective

Review how controls are monitored over time and how exceptions are identified, assessed, and escalated.

Key Findings

  • Monitoring thresholds were not consistently defined
  • Exception reviews lacked standard root-cause analysis
  • Evidence of monitoring varied in quality

Recommendations

  • Define thresholds for monitoring and escalation
  • Standardize exception review procedures
  • Align documentation requirements across teams

Skills Demonstrated

Control testing, exception monitoring, issue analysis, and operational reporting review.
