Enterprise Risk Register Implementation

Enterprise Risk Management Case Study

Problem

The organization lacked a centralized framework for identifying and monitoring operational and regulatory risks across departments. Leadership had limited visibility into emerging threats.

Approach

  • Designed institution-wide risk register
  • Aligned risk scoring with ERM methodology
  • Created impact × probability framework
  • Developed reporting dashboards for leadership

Framework

Each risk was evaluated using a structured scoring model.

  • Impact: Operational, financial, regulatory consequences
  • Probability: Likelihood of occurrence
  • Owner: Responsible department
  • Mitigation: Control strategies

Outcome

  • Tracked 50+ institutional risks
  • Reduced risk exposure by 20%
  • Improved leadership visibility through reporting dashboards
  • Standardized compliance documentation across departments
