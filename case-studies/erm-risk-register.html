Enterprise Risk Register

Case Study (Demo)

Problem

Leadership needed a centralized view of operational and regulatory risks, with clear ownership, prioritization, and action tracking.

What I Built

  • Risk register structure (owners, scoring, status, actions)

  • Mitigation tracking + reporting cadence
  • Executive-ready summaries

Outcome

  • Improved visibility into top risks and overdue mitigations
  • Standardized tracking across departments
  • Stronger audit readiness through documentation consistency
