Policy Compliance

Assessment of compliance alignment, oversight practices, and policy support in higher education environments.

Objective

Evaluate whether operational practices align with policy requirements and compliance expectations.

Key Findings

  • Policy updates were not consistently communicated
  • Review evidence was fragmented across departments
  • Compliance responsibilities were unevenly defined

Recommendations

  • Create consistent policy communication practices
  • Standardize compliance evidence retention
  • Clarify owners for major policy areas

Skills Demonstrated

Compliance analysis, policy review, oversight assessment, and control alignment evaluation.
