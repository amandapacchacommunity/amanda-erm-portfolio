Museum Risk Review

This case study reviews operational and visitor-facing risks within museum environments.

Objective

Identify major operational risks, evaluate control gaps, and support stronger governance and continuity planning.

Key Findings

  • Visitor-facing incident procedures were not consistently documented.
  • Escalation ownership varied across teams.
  • Continuity planning was not uniformly defined for critical functions.

Recommendations

  • Standardize incident documentation and escalation procedures.
  • Clarify roles for visitor services, operations, and leadership teams.
  • Strengthen continuity planning for high-priority operational areas.

Skills Demonstrated

Risk assessment, governance review, continuity analysis, and operational documentation.
