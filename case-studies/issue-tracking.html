Issue Tracking

Review of operational issue monitoring, corrective actions, and accountability for resolution.

Objective

Improve issue visibility, corrective action discipline, and accountability for timely resolution.

Key Findings

  • Issues were tracked in multiple locations
  • Closure evidence was inconsistent
  • Aging items were not escalated consistently

Recommendations

  • Centralize issue logs and reporting
  • Require documented evidence for closure
  • Escalate aging items based on defined thresholds

Skills Demonstrated

Issue management, corrective action tracking, operational reporting, and control follow-up review.
