Operational Risk Assessment

This case study reviews internal process risks and control gaps across operational workflows.

Objective

Identify workflow risks, evaluate control effectiveness, and recommend practical actions to improve execution quality.

Key Findings

  • Control ownership was not consistently assigned.
  • Issue visibility depended on informal reporting channels.
  • Workflow steps were not always aligned with documented procedures.

Recommendations

  • Assign risk and control owners for key workflows.
  • Centralize issue tracking and corrective action monitoring.
  • Update process documentation to reflect actual operating practices.

Skills Demonstrated

Operational risk assessment, workflow analysis, control review, and corrective action planning.
