Process Improvement

Workflow analysis focused on efficiency, clarity, and execution quality across operational processes.

Objective

Identify process inefficiencies and improve execution quality through clearer workflows and stronger operational design.

Key Findings

  • Duplicate steps increased delays
  • Role confusion affected workflow handoffs
  • Procedures did not always match actual practice

Recommendations

  • Simplify process steps and remove duplication
  • Clarify ownership for each workflow stage
  • Update procedures to reflect actual operations

Skills Demonstrated

Process mapping, workflow redesign, operational analysis, and execution improvement.
