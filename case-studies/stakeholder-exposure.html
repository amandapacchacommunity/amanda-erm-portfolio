Stakeholder Exposure

Review of reputation risk, stakeholder engagement exposure, and public communication considerations.

Objective

Assess institutional exposure related to public visibility, community engagement, and stakeholder expectations.

Key Findings

  • Communication escalation procedures were unclear
  • Stakeholder engagement risks were not formally documented
  • Reputation monitoring practices were inconsistent

Recommendations

  • Establish communication escalation protocols
  • Develop stakeholder exposure monitoring
  • Create response frameworks for public incidents

Skills Demonstrated

Reputation risk analysis, stakeholder governance review, and exposure assessment.
