Clery Act Compliance Program

Institutional compliance program supporting federal campus safety reporting requirements under the Clery Act.

Context

Served as Campus Safety Survey Administrator responsible for coordinating federal crime reporting and institutional safety disclosures affecting more than 10,000 campus stakeholders.

Questions That Informed the Work

  • How are crimes reported and classified across campus units?
  • Are Campus Security Authorities reporting incidents consistently?
  • How can safety data be consolidated across departments?
  • What controls ensure on-time federal reporting?

Methods Used

  • Compliance program administration
  • Cross-department data consolidation
  • Campus Security Authority training
  • Regulatory reporting analysis
  • Incident trend monitoring

KPIs

  • Annual Security Report submission deadline
  • CSA reporting participation rate
  • Crime log update timeliness
  • Incident classification accuracy

Stakeholders

  • Campus leadership
  • Student Affairs
  • Public Safety
  • Compliance offices
  • Federal regulators

Timeline

Annual compliance reporting cycle with quarterly monitoring.

Outcomes

  • 100% on-time federal reporting
  • 25+ Campus Security Authorities trained
  • Improved cross-department data reporting consistency

Skills Demonstrated

Regulatory compliance, campus safety governance, policy implementation, and cross-department coordination.
