Stakeholder Investigation Coordination

Cross-functional coordination model supporting investigations involving HR, legal, compliance, operations, and leadership stakeholders.

Objective

Improve coordination across stakeholders involved in complex investigative or compliance matters.

Context

Reflects work requiring collaboration across internal departments and external stakeholders to gather facts, align review, and support resolution.

Questions That Informed the Work

  • Which stakeholders need to be informed, consulted, or accountable?
  • How should information flow across teams?
  • Where do handoffs create delay or misalignment?
  • What structure supports confidentiality and timely coordination?

Methods Used

  • Stakeholder mapping
  • Coordination workflow review
  • Role clarification
  • Escalation and communication planning

KPIs / Measures Used

  • Response timeliness across stakeholders
  • Handoff completion rate
  • Escalation accuracy
  • Case coordination delay rate

Stakeholders / Teams Benefited

  • HR and employee relations
  • Legal and compliance teams
  • Operations leaders
  • Executive sponsors

Estimated Timeline

Typically 2–4 weeks depending on stakeholder count and issue complexity.

Key Findings

  • Coordination improved when roles were clarified up front
  • Handoffs were faster when communications were structured
  • Escalation quality improved when confidentiality boundaries were clear

Recommendations

  • Use stakeholder maps for complex matters
  • Clarify accountable, consulted, and informed roles
  • Define communication and escalation protocols early

Skills Demonstrated

Stakeholder management, investigation coordination, communication planning, and governance support.
