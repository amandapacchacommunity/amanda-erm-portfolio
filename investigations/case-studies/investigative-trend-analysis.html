Investigative Trend Analysis

Trend and theme analysis supporting leadership insight into case volume, recurring issues, and emerging risk patterns.

Objective

Improve leadership visibility into investigative activity by analyzing issue patterns, root causes, and repeat themes.

Context

Reflects data-driven reporting work using investigation outcomes, issue categories, and trend summaries to support risk mitigation.

Questions That Informed the Work

  • Which issue types recur most often?
  • Where are patterns emerging across teams or functions?
  • Which risks warrant preventative action?
  • How can reporting better inform leadership decisions?

Methods Used

  • Case categorization and theme grouping
  • Trend analysis
  • Root-cause review
  • Leadership summary reporting

KPIs / Measures Used

  • Case volume by category
  • Repeat incident rate
  • Average resolution time
  • Theme recurrence by function

Stakeholders / Teams Benefited

  • Executive leadership
  • HR and compliance teams
  • Operational managers
  • Governance reviewers

Estimated Timeline

Typically 2–4 weeks for data review, analysis, and reporting output.

Key Findings

  • Recurring categories became clearer when cases were grouped consistently
  • Trend reporting supported more proactive mitigation
  • Leadership summaries were stronger when root-cause themes were included

Recommendations

  • Use standardized issue taxonomy
  • Track recurring themes over time
  • Integrate trend reporting into governance reviews

Skills Demonstrated

Trend analysis, thematic reporting, investigative data interpretation, and executive communication.
